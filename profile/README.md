## Open real packs. Build real value.

PocketPull is the ultimate gacha experience for collectible cards — where every pack open does more than give you a card.

Rip live inventory-backed packs, track your collection, chase binder milestones, and grow your account through quests, referrals, and rewards. Keep your hits, ship them, or sell back when an offer is available.

## Why PocketPull?

| | |
| :---: | --- |
| **Pocket Points** | Earn points through purchases, quests, and referrals. |
| **Account Levels** | The more you play, the more your account grows. |
